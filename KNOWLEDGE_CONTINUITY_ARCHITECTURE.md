# Knowledge Continuity Architecture

## Layers

- Knowledge Archive Layer
- Pattern Archive Layer
- Narrative Archive Layer
- Guidance Archive Layer

## What FamilieKompas Learns

Generalized support patterns, guidance strategies, reflection methods, and resource gaps.

## What Hermes Inherits

Validated narrative patterns and conversation strategies.

## What Family ARC Inherits

Specialized family-support knowledge and safety-aware methodology.

## What Ryzen Receives

Aggregated, non-personal specialization intelligence.

## Convergence Boundary

Knowledge continuity is future infrastructure. The current product may produce learning signals, but no automated knowledge synchronization, memory federation, Ryzen feed, or ARC infrastructure should be implemented during Phase 6.
