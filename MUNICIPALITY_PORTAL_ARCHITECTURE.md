# Municipality Portal Architecture

## Purpose

Help municipalities understand local family-support needs and resource gaps.

## Future Capabilities

- Local resources
- Trend reporting
- Referral visibility
- Support gap detection
- Funding readiness
- Community programs

## Data Boundary

Municipal reporting should use aggregated and anonymized insights, not identifiable family stories.

## Convergence Boundary

Municipality deployments require multi-tenant governance, anonymization standards, and resource governance. They remain design-only until the private beta produces enough non-identifying support-gap evidence.
