# Partner Portal Architecture

## Users

Psychologists, mediators, coaches, social workers, parenting specialists, and related professionals.

## Future Capabilities

- Profile
- Languages
- Region
- Specialization
- Availability
- Referral intake
- Referral tracking
- Outcome tracking
- Marketplace readiness

## Boundaries

Partner visibility must not imply endorsement without validation. Referrals need consent and clear role boundaries.
