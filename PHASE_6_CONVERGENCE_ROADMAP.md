# Phase 6 Convergence Roadmap

## Controlling Principle

Conversation first. Value first. Trust first. Contact details later. Account later. Subscription later.

## Now

- DESIGN.md.
- Human experience blueprint.
- Hermes integration blueprint.
- Private beta polish plan.
- Founder workflow polish plan.

## Next

- Visual redesign implementation.
- Prototype Hermes behind the existing conversation service boundary.
- Improved private beta.
- Manual referral preparation.
- Better founder review flow only if founder workload requires it.

## Later

- Family Portal.
- Accounts.
- Subscriptions.
- Partner Portal.
- School Portal.
- Municipality Portal.
- Association Portal.
- CRM.
- Multi-tenant architecture.
- Knowledge continuity tooling.
- ARC maturity work.

## Do Not Build Yet

- Accounts.
- Payments.
- Portals.
- School systems.
- Municipality systems.
- Partner marketplace.
- Full CRM.
- Multi-tenant infrastructure.
- Production Hermes integration.
- Ryzen integration.
- ARC infrastructure.

## Implementation Gate

No future layer should move from architecture to production until it has evidence from private beta usage, founder workflow pain, repeated family return behavior, or validated partner/organization demand.
