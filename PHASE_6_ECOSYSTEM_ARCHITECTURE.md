# Phase 6 Ecosystem Architecture

## Objective

Evolve FamilieKompas from a one-time conversation assistant into a family-support ecosystem while preserving the order:

Conversation first. Value first. Trust first. Relationship second. Contact details later. Account creation later. Subscription later.

## Ecosystem Layers

- Family Portal: identity, memory, history, action plans, privacy controls.
- Founder Attention Center: mission priority, crisis, follow-up, outcomes, workload protection.
- Partner Portal: professional profiles, referrals, availability, outcomes.
- School Portal: referral requests, parent collaboration, safeguarding boundaries.
- Municipality Portal: local resources, trends, support gaps, programs.
- Association Portal: intake, volunteer readiness, coordination.

## Non-Negotiables

- Safety remains separate from growth or monetization.
- User consent precedes retention.
- Personal data and knowledge archives stay separate.
- MVP simplicity remains protected until product-market fit.
