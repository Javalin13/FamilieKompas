# Referral Ecosystem

## Flows

- Family -> Founder -> Partner
- Family -> School
- Family -> Municipality
- Family -> Association
- Partner -> Family
- School -> Family
- Municipality -> Family

## Referral Objects

Future referrals should include consent, summary, urgency, themes, preferred contact, status, and outcome.

## Boundaries

No referral should happen without informed consent. Emergency concerns follow safety protocols first.
