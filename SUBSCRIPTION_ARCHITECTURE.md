# Subscription Architecture

## Free Tier

Initial conversation, basic guidance, safety resources.

## Support Tier

Saved guidance, follow-up reminders, lightweight continuity.

## Premium Family Tier

Deeper plans, archive, periodic reviews, human review options.

## Professional Tier

Profile, referral intake, availability, outcome tracking.

## Municipality Tier

Local resources, aggregated trends, support gap visibility.

## Boundary

Never monetize crisis access or emergency guidance.
